Data Scientist

Job Summary

We are the Mac Systems Quality team, and we are looking for a highly motivated and detail-oriented data scientist to own the data analytics and visualization of Mac power data. As a member of the team, you'll work closely with internal teams to evaluate complex data, analyze data from multiple angles, build analytical workflows, create impactful visualizations, and deliver findings that directly impact the business. Do you have an analytical mind and enjoy solving challenging problems in a fast paced and dynamic environment? You can help to drive and improve the quality of future Apple products that will delight and inspire millions of Apple’s customers every single day!

Key Qualifications

    - Experience producing meaningful visualizations and dashboards using tools such as Tableau.
    - Experience with SQL and NoSQL (MongoDB, etc) databases and creating data pipelines.
    - Solid background in data science and data mining, including the ability to model, interpret and present data into clear and concise takeaways that drive action.
    - Excellent written and verbal communication skills and experience presenting data to cross-functional teams and/or management.
    - Understanding of computer hardware, power fundamentals, and machine learning is a plus. Experience with Splunk is also a plus.

Description

In this role you will use your deep knowledge of data extraction, exploration, and analysis to produce reports and visualizations of critical power metrics, enabling partners from across the company to gain real-time insights into critical power validation status. In this role you will:

    - Use statistical tools and models to identify, analyze, and interpret patterns and trends in data sets that could be effective for the identification and diagnosis of key issues
    - Build dashboards, tools, and reports to present data associated with product performance to drive informed decision-making across all levels of the company
    - Develop and implement scalable and flexible software tools that will enhance data gathering, evaluation, processing and analysis

Education & Experience

BS or MS in Statistics, Data Mining, Machine Learning, Analytics, Applied Math, or Computer Science.
